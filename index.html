Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a website cost?
Typically, our projects range from ₹4,000 to ₹7,000 depending on the complexity (e.g., simple informational site vs. complex dashboard). You can use our AI Estimation Tool above to get a clearer idea based on your specific needs.
How long will it take to build?
We usually complete standard websites within one week. Simpler projects can be done much faster—for example, we built our anime site AniVlyx in just a single day. If you need custom features or complex debugging, it might take a bit longer, but we'll keep you updated daily.
How big is your team?
We are a lean, highly experienced team of 3 skilled developers. We've built numerous complex projects (streaming platforms, school management systems, AI tools), so while we are small, we deliver quality that rivals larger agencies.
Is this a one-time cost?
Yes. Our development fee is a one-time payment. You will only face recurring costs if you choose to buy a custom domain (like .com) or paid hosting from third parties. If you stick to our recommended stack (Cloudflare/Firebase free tiers), you pay ₹0 monthly after we hand over the project.
How much does a domain cost?
If you want a custom domain (e.g., yourbusiness.com), it usually costs between ₹1,000 - ₹3,000 per year, payable to registrars like GoDaddy or Hostinger. However, we also offer free subdomains (like .web.app or .pages.dev) that are free forever with no limits—a great option if you are just starting out.
What is the difference between Static and Dynamic websites?

Think of a Static Website like a digital brochure. It shows the same information to everyone who visits (images, text, contact info). It is incredibly fast, secure, and usually free to host. Perfect for portfolios and landing pages.

Think of a Dynamic Website like a smart app (e.g., Facebook or a Dashboard). It changes based on who is looking at it, saves user data, allows logins, and handles complex tasks. We build both types depending on what you need.
